How to Play

Buzzer Mode (Toss-Ups)

  1. Turn on Buzzer mode.
  2. Press Start Toss-Up to begin Toss-Up #1 (then #2, #3).
  3. Letters reveal automatically; anyone can buzz by tapping their player card.
  4. Buzzed player types an answer and hits Solve.
  5. Wrong solve locks out for 5 seconds.
  6. If time expires, the Toss-Up ends and the next begins automatically.
